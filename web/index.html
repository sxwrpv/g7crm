Revenue leaks

Where local businesses quietly lose money.

Instead of vague feature lists, we work the leaks first.

Leak 01

Missed calls

Phone rings, no answer, customer moves on. We add an instant response path so the lead is not lost.

Call missed → text-back → reply captured → booking
Leak 02

Slow follow-up

Lead comes in, nobody follows up properly, and the opportunity goes cold. We create clear follow-up logic with ownership.

Lead in → SLA timer → task created → status tracked
Leak 03

No-shows and drop-off

Appointments slip because reminders are weak or inconsistent. We put confirmation and reminder flows in place.

Booked → reminder → confirm → attend
Leak 04

Dead customer lists

Old customers sit unused. We build reactivation and review sequences that turn past jobs into more revenue.

Past customer → check-in → offer → repeat work
Workflows

Practical systems, not generic automations.

Workflow A

Lead capture and routing

  • Trigger: website form, phone call, or message
  • Action: capture details and assign status
  • Handoff: send into G7CRM and task queue
  • Outcome: nothing sits invisible
Workflow B

Missed call recovery

  • Trigger: call missed or after-hours contact
  • Action: instant text-back with next step
  • Handoff: alert owner or office when reply lands
  • Outcome: fewer lost enquiries
Workflow C

Booking and reminders

  • Trigger: job or appointment confirmed
  • Action: reminder and confirmation sequence
  • Handoff: internal alert if customer does not confirm
  • Outcome: fewer no-shows and cleaner diaries
Workflow D

Reactivation and reviews

  • Trigger: finished job or inactive customer list
  • Action: review request or reactivation message
  • Handoff: replies logged back into the CRM
  • Outcome: more repeat business from existing demand
